Top Selling Cars in the United States

Summary

This study was conducted to invesigate the top selling cars in the United States. In our analysis, you will find graphs comparing the top 50 new and used cars in the United States to see if there is any bias between new and used. You will also find a graph that compares the top 50 popular cars between the states of New York and California, to see if there is any regional bias. Finally we took 2 of each high, middle, and low-end cars to compare sale trends from 2014 to 2019 with Lexus, Mercedes-Benz, Toyota, Honda, General Motors, and Kia.
